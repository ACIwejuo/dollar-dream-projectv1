    How to Open a Bank Account

    1. Choose a Bank and Account : Pick a bank and decide whether you want a checking account (for spending) or a savings account (for saving).
    2. Gather your documents:
      • A valid ID (like a passport or driver's license).
      • Your Social Security Number (if required).
      • A parent or guardian (if under 18).
    3. Visit the Bank or Apply Online: Go to branch or visit the bank's website to fill out application.
    4. Make a Deposit: Add the required minimum deposit (often $25 - $100) to activate your account.
    5. Set up Online Banking: Sign up for online or mobile banking to manage your account.
